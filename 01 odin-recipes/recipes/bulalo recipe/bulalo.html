Bulalo Recipe

It’s a well known fact that Filipinos love stew and soup dishes. From the sour sinigang to the sweet tinola, there’s guaranteed to be a soup dish for every flavor palate preference –– all great to sip and enjoy, especially during long, cool nights. But one of the most popular, and arguably most delicious, soups out there, is the classic Bulalo, or beef shank stew.

Ingredients:

  • 1 kg Beef Shanks
  • 1/2 Cabbage
  • 1 bundle Bok Choy
  • 2 cobs corn cut into 3 pieces
  • 2 tablespoons whole peppercorn
  • 50 g green onion cut into 1 1/2 inch pieces
  • 1 L water
  • Fish Sauce to taste

Instructions

  1. In a big cooking pot, bring water to a boil
  2. Put in beef shanks followed by the onion and whole peppercorn then simmer for 1.5 hours (30 mins if using pressure cooker) or until meat is tender
  3. Add the corn and simmer for 10 mins
  4. Add the fish sauce, cabbage, bok choy and green onion
  5. Serve hot. Share and enjoy!
